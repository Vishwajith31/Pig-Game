Here are the rules for the game

  • GOAL: Be the first to reach 100 points.
  • PLAYERS: Two players take turns.
      • TURNS:
    • Roll the dice.
    • Get points for numbers other than 1.
    • If you roll a 1, you lose your turn.
  • HOLD: Click "Hold" to add your current points to your total and switch to the other player.
  • WINNING: First to 100 points wins.
  • NEW GAME: Start a new game anytime.
